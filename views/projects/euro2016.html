Development

They were two major components for this project. Finding free API that will update the brackets, results and give some basic information for the teams playing (football-data.org was the perfect choice for that). Second one was the ability each competitor to fill its own bets before the start of the game and automatically after the game to comapre them to the correct score and deduct points. I used Firebase (Realtime DB from Google) to store bets and manage accounts, without a single line of SQL. Also added some stats for the games such as average goals, how many games were played until now and of course average successful bets.

Info

The idea for this site came couple of days before the start of the Euro 2016. As usual my dad and I wanted to keep bets for the games and instead of pen and paper or excel sheet decided to go on the web. Eventually even my mum decided that she wants to play. We were betting only on correct score and average around 11% successful bets.
